about us

The International Day of Yoga has been celebrated annually on 21 june since 2015, following its inception in the United Nations General Assembly in 2014. Yoga is a physical ,mental and spirtual practice which originated in India. The Indian priminister, Narendra Modi , in his UN adress suggested the date of 21 june, as it is the longest day of the year in northern Hemisphere and shares a special significance in many parts of the world.
